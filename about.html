About Studio Ghibli

Welcome to Ghibli Studio, a world-renowned animation powerhouse nestled in the heart of Japan's creative landscape. Since its inception in 1985 by visionary directors Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata, Ghibli Studio has enchanted audiences worldwide with its timeless tales, breathtaking animation, and profound storytelling.

Our Films

From the whimsical wonders of "My Neighbor Totoro" to the epic adventures of "Princess Mononoke," each Ghibli film is a masterpiece crafted with meticulous attention to detail and a deep respect for the art of animation. Our diverse repertoire encompasses stories of love, friendship, courage, and the beauty of the natural world, resonating with audiences of all ages.

Our Team

Behind every Ghibli film is a team of passionate artists, animators, writers, and storytellers dedicated to bringing Miyazaki and Takahata's vision to life. With boundless creativity and unwavering dedication, our talented staff continues to push the boundaries of animation, inspiring wonder and awe with each new project.

Our Legacy

Over the years, Ghibli Studio has left an indelible mark on the world of animation, earning accolades and awards for its groundbreaking work. From the iconic characters of "Spirited Away" to the haunting beauty of "Grave of the Fireflies," our films have captured the imagination of audiences and critics alike, standing the test of time as timeless classics.

Our Future

As we embark on the next chapter of our journey, Ghibli Studio remains committed to fostering creativity, diversity, and innovation in storytelling. With new projects on the horizon and a commitment to upholding the values that have defined us for decades, we look forward to continuing to inspire and delight audiences for years to come.

Thank you for joining us on this magical adventure. Welcome to Ghibli Studio, where dreams take flight and imagination knows no bounds.
